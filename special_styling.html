During a stage in my life, I needed to transition from my corporate attire during work hours and floral dresses during leisure time, to a more versatile and easy-to-wear style. Edna surprised me by first wanting to understand me as a person, not just my daily role. As we sorted through my closet, I was apprehensive about having to let go of my favorite pieces. Edna goes beyond just putting together an outfit. She taught me to view each item as an ingredient that can be used in various combinations, making them incredibly satisfying and appealing, resulting in compliments from others
